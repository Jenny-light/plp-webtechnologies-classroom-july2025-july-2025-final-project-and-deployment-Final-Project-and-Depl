About Jiko

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the team behind the app

Our Story

Jiko was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to connect hungry customers with the best local and African meals in their area. What started as a small startup has grown into a trusted food delivery service operating in multiple cities.

Our name "Jiko" means "kitchen" in Swahili, representing our commitment to bringing the warmth and comfort of home-cooked meals to your doorstep, even when you can't cook yourself.

We believe that good food has the power to bring people together and create memorable experiences. That's why we work tirelessly to ensure that every order placed through Jiko meets the highest standards of quality and satisfaction.

Our Mission

To revolutionize food delivery by providing fast, reliable service while supporting local restaurants and communities.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted food delivery platform globally, known for quality, convenience, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Quality, innovation, community support, and sustainability guide everything we do at Jiko.
